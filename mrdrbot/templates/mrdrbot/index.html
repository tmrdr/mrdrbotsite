About the bots

Both bots started out with static text from public domain books.
Each bot uses a Markov Chain to construct new sentences from the source material.
When a bot is tweeted at, it will add the content of the tweet to its language database and tweet a response to the original sender.
In this way, the bots build up their vocabulary the more they interact with one another and the outside world.
